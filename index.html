About Surti Omelet

Surti Omelet is an ode to Surat’s love for buttery, cheesy and spicy egg dishes – crafted by Munna Surti with his own signature style.

  • Fresh eggs cooked live in front of you with generous butter, cheese and our secret Surti masala.
  • From classic butter omelet to loaded green cheese crush and masala half fry – something for every egg lover.
  • Perfect for late-night cravings, quick dinners, and casual meets with friends & family.

Every plate at Surti Omelet is served hot with soft pav / bun, extra butter and a punch of flavour. Whether you like it mild and cheesy or fiery and spicy, Munna Surti makes sure your omelet tastes just “dhamaka” in every bite.

Our Story – From Street Cart to Surti Favourite

What started as a small egg cart run by Munna Surti has today become a go-to place for omelet lovers.

Munna began by serving simple butter omelets outside a busy chowk. With time, he started experimenting – adding green chutney, layering cheese, creating “crush” omelets and masala half fry that people kept coming back for.

Word-of-mouth turned Surti Omelet into a local brand. Today, the same honest approach remains: hot, fresh, flavourful food, served fast, with a smile. No show-off – only taste and consistency.

If you’ve ever shared one plate of crush omelet with friends at midnight – you already know what Surti Omelet is all about.

Green Cheese Surti Omelet Special

A fully loaded plate for true cheese lovers – inspired by Surat’s favourite style.

Double-egg omelet whipped with coriander and green chutney, stuffed with juicy onions and tomatoes, layered with cheese and finished with a drizzle of butter and masala. Served with two butter pav, sliced onions and lemon so you can adjust the tang to your taste.

Crush Omelet Power Plate

All the flavours of gravy, cheese and masala on one big sharing plate.

Thick omelet chopped and tossed in spicy tomato-onion gravy, loaded with cheese and finished with extra butter. Served with four pav – perfect for two hungry friends or one very hungry foodie.

Surti Masala Half Fry Combo

Runny yolks, spicy gravy and soft pav – classic Surti-style comfort food.

Two eggs half-fried and topped with a rich masala of onion, tomato and secret spices, finished with cheese and coriander. Comes with pav, salad and chutney – an ideal heavy snack or mini-meal.

Egg Rice Masala Meal

One bowl meal for lunch or late-night hunger.

Flavoured rice tossed with scrambled egg, capsicum, onions and Surti spices, served with raita and salad. Light on the stomach but loaded with flavour and protein.

Midnight Hungry Pack

Perfect for hostel groups and night-shift gangs.

One crush omelet, one cheese bhurji, four pav and chai for two – a value pack made for sharing, ideal for late-night cravings and weekend hangouts.
